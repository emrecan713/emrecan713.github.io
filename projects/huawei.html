On this page, I present my journey as a Tech Planning Specialist at Huawei, introducing my diverse roles and achievements through a visual storytelling approach.

Industry Huawei Tech Planning

Role Overview

As a Tech Planning Specialist at Huawei, I was involved in the intersection of technology and user experience, working on products that required deep understanding of human-machine interaction principles. My role encompassed strategic planning, product analysis, and cross-functional collaboration across multiple product lines.

Product Ecosystem

I contributed to Huawei's diverse product ecosystem, from consumer electronics to automotive HiCar integration. My work focused on understanding user needs and translating them into actionable technical specifications for the development teams.

Impact

Through my role, I developed a comprehensive understanding of how technology can be designed to work seamlessly with human cognition and behavior, bridging the gap between complex engineering systems and intuitive user experiences.
