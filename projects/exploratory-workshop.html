Co-design Workshop

Exploratory Deformation / Student Interaction Design Conference 2018

Workshop SiDer 2018 Co-Design

In my first year of pursuing my PhD degree, I took charge of arranging and managing a co-design workshop at SiDer 2018, alongside some Master's students. The workshop was aimed at discovering design opportunities through deforming sheet materials, which had varying physical properties. It was my first experience of organizing a workshop, and it taught me a lot about preparing, presenting, and delivering design briefs.
