UX Seat Booking App

As part of the Google UX Design course, I designed a seat booking app for a movie theater, which allowed me to apply my research knowledge and improve my UX design skills.

UX Design Google UX Certificate Mobile App

Research & Discovery

These are the preliminary sketches that outline the fundamental features of the app along with the user flow. The sketches also include information regarding the intended audience of the app.

User interviews were conducted to understand the needs and pain points of moviegoers. Two different personas were created based on the interview findings.

User Journey Mapping

User journey maps were created to visualize the end-to-end experience of booking a movie seat, identifying key touchpoints and opportunities for improvement.
